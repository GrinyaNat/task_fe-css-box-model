Basic document flow

I am a basic block level element. My adjacent block level elements sit on
new lines below me.

By default we span 100% of the width of our parent element, and we are as
tall as our child content. Our total width and height is our content + padding
+border width/height.

We are separated by our margins. Because of margin collapsing, we are
separated by the width of one of our margins, not both.

adjacent text nodes, if there is space on the same line. Overflowing inline
